Simple, Yet Powerful

Gmail has two hidden tricks. We make them easy to use.

The "Dot" Trick

Gmail ignores dots in your username. We generate every possible combination, like j.o.h.n@gmail.com from john@gmail.com.

The "Plus" Trick

Add a "+" and any word after your username to create unique aliases for different services, like john+shopping@gmail.com.
